How To Play The Game

The object of the game is to defeat all of your opponents in single combat.

The first thing you need to do is select your avatar by clicking on any of the available choices. Once you have selected your avatar, that selection is moved to the first position on the left, and you are unable to click on it again.

Once your avatar is selected, select your first opponent. Again, simply click on one of the available choices.

As soon as you have selected your opponent, the fight appears. Images for your avatar and opponent along with a health status bar for each fighter. Click the "fight" button to start the action and see the results of the round. Keep clicking on the "fight" button until you or your opponent has been defeated.

If you successfully defeat your opponent, you will be given the opportunity to select your next opponent. This time, you will only see the choices you have remaining.

Good Luck!
